Private Events & Celebrations

Create memorable experiences in our beautiful private dining spaces

Private Dining Room

Our intimate private dining room accommodates up to 20 guests with elegant decor and a dedicated service team. Perfect for corporate dinners, milestone celebrations, and family gatherings.

  • Customizable multi-course menus
  • Wine pairing options
  • Audio-visual equipment available
  • Private wait staff

Garden Terrace

Our seasonal outdoor garden terrace provides a picturesque setting for up to 40 guests. Available from May through October, weather permitting, it's ideal for cocktail receptions, summer celebrations, and garden parties.

  • Standing reception or seated dining
  • Flexible layout options
  • Ambient string lighting
  • Covered canopy available

Full Restaurant Buyout

For the ultimate private event, reserve our entire restaurant for up to 120 guests. Full restaurant buyouts include customized menus, personalized service, and exclusive use of all facilities.

  • Complete menu customization

  • Custom decor options
  • Tailored bar and beverage program
